---
title: "General Settings"
description: "Configure your company's main details in Vayu, including name, contacts, logo, address, tax ID, and banking information for invoices."
---

This is where you can enter your company’s main details:

- Company name
- Main contact
- Upload a company logo (PNG format)
- Full address
- Tax ID
- Bank information
  - Bank name
  - Bank address
  - IBAN (routing) number
  - Swift code
  - Account number
  - ABA

Once done, hit the blue **Save** button in the upper righthand corner of the page.
