---
title: "Company Settings"
description: "Configure your company's general settings in Vayu, including contact details, logo, address, tax ID, and bank information used on invoices and reports."
icon: "door-open"
---

This is where you can enter your company’s main details:

- Company name
- Main contact
- Upload a company logo (PNG format)
- Full address
- Tax ID
- Bank information
  - Bank name
  - Bank address
  - IBAN (routing) number
  - Swift code
  - Account number
  - ABA

Once done, hit the blue **Save** button in the upper righthand corner of the page.
