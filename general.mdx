title: "Company Settings"
description: "Set your company name, contact, logo, address, tax ID, and bank details, including IBAN, Swift code, and ABA, to populate invoices and integrations."
icon: "door-open"
---

This is where you can enter your company’s main details:

- Company name
- Main contact
- Upload a company logo (PNG format)
- Full address
- Tax ID
- Bank information
  - Bank name
  - Bank address
  - IBAN (routing) number
  - Swift code
  - Account number
  - ABA

Once done, hit the blue **Save** button in the upper righthand corner of the page.
