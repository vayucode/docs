---
title: "Exporting Invoices"
---

You can export invoice data from multiple locations depending on your workflow needs.

### From monthly closure

To export finalized monthly invoices:

1. Navigate to **Invoices** \> **Monthly Closure**
2. Select the billing cycle you want to export
3. Review and approve invoices for the period
4. Click **Export to CSV**

Once invoices are approved, they become locked for editing and ready for export. This ensures data integrity when syncing to your ERP system or maintaining financial records.

### From real-time invoices

To export current consumption data before the billing cycle closes:

1. Navigate to **Invoices** \> **Real-time**
2. View current consumption for any customer
3. Click **Export** to download invoice data

Real-time exports allow you to:

- Analyze current usage before month-end
- Make commitment and credit follow-up decisions
- Identify potential overages early
- Engage with customer service and sales teams before invoice issuance
- Download, export, or sync current invoice summaries

### Invoice export contents

Invoice exports include:

- Customer information
- Invoice period and dates
- Line items with product details
- Usage consumption data
- Pricing calculations (tiered, volume, per unit, revenue share)
- Commitment tracking
- Credit applications
- Total amounts and payment status
