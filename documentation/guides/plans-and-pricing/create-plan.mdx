Plans hold the pricing, commercials, and terms defined for your customers. Vayu offers three ways to start a new plan, each surfaced from a single creation dialog so you can choose the right path without leaving your workflow.

## Start the creation flow

1. Select **Plans and Pricing** from the left-hand navigation.
2. Click **Create** in the upper-right corner.
3. The **Create a new pricing plan** dialog opens with three options. Select one and click **Next**.

## Choose a creation method

Pick the option that best matches what you are doing:

### Start a new contract

Choose this option to create a custom plan directly tied to a specific customer:

1. Select **Start a new contract** and click **Next**.
2. In the contract dialog, select the customer and configure the contract terms.
3. Add fees and products as needed.
4. Save the contract to assign it to the customer immediately.

### Create a new template

Choose this option to build a reusable plan that can be assigned to multiple customers later:

1. Select **Create a new template** and click **Next**.
2. **Name the plan** (for example, "Premium plan") and add an optional description.
3. Click **Add Fees** to view the pricing elements menu.
4. Choose fixed fees, usage charges, or any combination. You can add as many as you like, one at a time.
5. For each product, enter the product name and the amount to be charged.
6. To add more line items, select **Add Item** and fill in the required details. Each item becomes a line item on invoices issued from this plan.
7. Define the **plan setting details** — start date, duration, and billing interval.
8. Click **Save Plan and Continue** at the bottom right to save your selections.

<Note>
  After saving, the system redirects you to the plan that was created.
</Note>

### Choose a template

Choose this option to apply an existing template to a customer:

1. Select **Choose a template** and click **Next**.
2. Browse the list of existing templates and select the one you want to use.
3. Confirm the selection to start a contract from that template.
4. Adjust any customer-specific details and save.

## Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Billing interval">
    The frequency with which invoices are sent to customers for the products or services they have used.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plan duration">
    The timeframe of your plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Auto-renewal">
    The ability to automatically extend the contract duration for another term.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Activation / Deactivation">
    Changing the status of the plan to inactive prevents the issuing of future invoices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Start date">
    Billing cycles and invoices are triggered based on the start date given for the plan. Future or past dates can be used. If the plan is active, the billing cycle automation flow generates invoices accordingly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
