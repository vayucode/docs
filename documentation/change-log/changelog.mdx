<Update label="April 21, 2026" tags={["Reporting","Billing & Invoicing","Usage Metering"]}>
  # New report library: accounting, billing, and usage

  Vayu's reports library has been expanded with a new set of reports organized into three categories.

  ## New

  **Accounting reports** for closing books and recognizing revenue:

  - **Month-End** — consolidated invoice, revenue, and payment data for the period, with CSV export and ERP sync.
  - **Recurring revenue (MRR/ARR)** — recurring revenue tracked at customer and account level with custom fields and contract-phase support.
  - **Revenue** — recognized revenue with a redesigned waterfall chart and filters.
  - **Contracts** — list of contracts with TCV, ACV, renewal dates, and estimated bookings.

  <Frame>
    ![Revenue Waterfall](/images/revenue-waterfall-chart-20260421-130623.gif)
  </Frame>

  See [Accounting Reports](https://guide.withvayu.com/accounting-reports).

  **Billing reports** for monitoring invoices and AR:

  - **Billing forecast** — upcoming invoices and projected charges before they're issued.
  - **AR Health** — overdue invoices grouped by aging buckets to support collections.
  - **Email exception** — failed invoice email deliveries with failure type and contact details.
  - **Customers** — directory of customers with billing status, contracts, and outstanding balance.

  See [Billing Reports](https://guide.withvayu.com/billing-reports).

  **Usage and data reports** for understanding consumption:

  - **Commitment vs. usage** — actual usage compared against contractual commitments and overages.
  - **Uncharged usage** — usage events that have not been billed, to prevent revenue leakage.
  - **Anonymous customers** — usage from unattributed customers ready to be linked.
  - **Usage by product** — usage broken down by product or pricing component.
  - **Usage report** — granular event-level breakdown of usage and charges.

  See [Usage and Data Reports](https://guide.withvayu.com/usage-and-data-reports).

  ## Updates

  - **Invoices Portal** (formerly Customer Portal) — renamed and refocused around the invoice experience. See [Invoices Portal](https://guide.withvayu.com/documentation/guides/invoices/invoices-portal).
  - **Subsidiaries** (formerly Entities) — manage multiple legal entities and sync them with NetSuite from a single dropdown.
</Update>
