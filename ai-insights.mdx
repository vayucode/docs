AI insights provide intelligent analysis of your customer data, revenue patterns, and business metrics. The system automatically generates reports and recommendations based on your account activity.

## Overview

AI insights analyze your Vayu data to surface meaningful patterns and trends. These insights help you understand customer behavior, identify revenue opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

## Accessing AI insights

To access AI insights:

1. Navigate to **Reports** in the left-side menu
2. Select **Ask Agent** from the reports dashboard
   <Frame>
     ![Ask Agent](/images/ask-agent.png)
   </Frame>
3. View generated insights and recommendations

## Types of insights

### Customer insights

AI analyzes customer data to identify:

- High-value customers and growth opportunities
- Customers at risk of churn
- Usage patterns and trends
- Revenue concentration and diversification

<Frame>
  ![Churn Rate](/images/churn-rate.gif)
</Frame>

### Revenue insights

The system provides analysis on:

- Revenue trends and forecasting
- MRR and ARR patterns
- Contract renewal predictions
- Pricing optimization opportunities

  ![Mrr Arr Ai Agent Question](/images/mrr-arr-ai-agent-question.gif)

### Usage insights

AI examines usage data to surface:

- Product adoption rates
- Feature utilization patterns
- Consumption trends
- Capacity planning recommendations

  ![Customer Analysis Revenue](/images/customer-analysis-revenue.gif)

## Generated reports

AI insights automatically generate reports that can be:

- Viewed in the Vayu dashboard
- Downloaded as CSV files
- Scheduled for regular delivery
- Customized with additional fields

For more information on standard reports, see the [<u>Enhanced Dashboard</u>](https://guide.withvayu.com/documentation/change-log/changelog#enhanced-dashboard) section in the changelog.

## Best practices

- Review AI insights regularly to stay informed about your business trends
- Use insights to inform pricing and packaging decisions
- Combine AI insights with custom reports for comprehensive analysis
- Act on churn risk indicators proactively
