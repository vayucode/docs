## Exporting Reports

### Recurring revenue reports

To export MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) or ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) reports:

1. Navigate to **Reports** on the left-side menu
2. Click the menu on the right
3. Select **Recurring Revenue Reports \>**
4. Choose between:
   - **Account-level view** - Aggregated revenue by account
   - **Customer-level view** - Revenue broken down by individual customer
5. Click **Download** to export as CSV

### What's included in MRR reports

MRR reports include:

- Recurring revenue by customer or account
- Custom fields as additional columns
- Contract phase information
- Historical MRR data for trend analysis
- ARR calculations and metrics

These reports are viewable in the Vayu UI and can be downloaded for external analysis or integration with financial reporting tools.

### Dashboard and revenue reports

The Vayu dashboard displays comprehensive revenue data including:

- Real-time ARR graph on the homepage
- Revenue chart data
- Suggested reports organized by section
- Customer-specific revenue metrics

You can export data from these views for deeper analysis or to share with stakeholders.
